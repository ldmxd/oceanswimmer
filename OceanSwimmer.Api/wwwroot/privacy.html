Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

OceanSwimmer (oceanswimmer.com.au) is a community website that tracks publicly available ocean swim race results. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or sign in, we may collect:

  • Email address — used to identify your account and send transactional emails (e.g. email verification).
  • Name — your first and last name, used to match and display your race results.
  • OAuth provider ID — a unique identifier from Google or Facebook if you choose to sign in via those services. We do not receive your social media password.
  • Claimed race results — the race results you choose to associate with your account.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any sensitive personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Identify you when you log in
  • Display your claimed race results on your profile
  • Send account-related emails (e.g. email verification)
  • Match your name against publicly available race result data

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any automated decision-making.

3. Third-Party Sign-In (Google & Facebook)

You may sign in using your Google or Facebook account. When you do, we receive only your email address and a unique identifier from that provider. We do not receive access to your social media posts, contacts, or any other account data. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and Facebook's Privacy Policy for information on how those services handle your data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to operate the service (e.g. our hosting provider DigitalOcean and email provider SendGrid).

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing drinkwater.mark@gmail.com.

6. Cookies

OceanSwimmer uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

7. Security

Passwords are stored as secure bcrypt hashes and are never stored in plain text. All data is transmitted over HTTPS.

8. Children's Privacy

OceanSwimmer is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at drinkwater.mark@gmail.com.
